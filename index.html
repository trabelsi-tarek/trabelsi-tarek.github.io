I am a Research Associate at the Department of Earth and Environmental Sciences at the University of Pennsylvania. My research focuses on using computational techniques to explore the photochemical mechanisms of formation and destruction, the electronic structure, and the spectroscopy of molecular species expected or detected in the interstellar medium (ISM) and planetary atmospheres. My work involves detailed analysis and simulation to uncover the properties and behaviors of these molecules, contributing to our understanding of complex astronomical environments.
