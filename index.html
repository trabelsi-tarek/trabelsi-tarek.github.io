About me

I received my BSc and MSc from the University of Tunis El Manar. In 2016, I earned a PhD in Physics through a joint program between the University of Paris Est-Marne la Vallée and the University of Tunis El Manar. Following my PhD, I joined the University of Nebraska-Lincoln as a postdoctoral fellow, where I worked until 2018. Subsequently, I moved to the University of Pennsylvania as a postdoctoral associate. Since July 2023, I have been a research associate at the University of Pennsylvania. My research interests focus on the theoretical molecular spectroscopy of small molecules and their applications in astrophysics and physical chemistry.
